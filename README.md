# Water — three small instruments for a living thing

The water in you and the water on the earth are the same water. It only borrows a body for a while, then returns to the river, the cloud, the sea. These are three quiet tools for tending it.

**Free, private, and fully offline.** No accounts, no tracking, no build step. Nothing you do is ever sent anywhere — every page is a single self-contained HTML file.

🌊 **Live site:** https://yonatanmost-commits.github.io/water/

## The three pieces

| | | |
|---|---|---|
| 🌙 **The Companion** — *be present* | [`water.html`](water.html) | A night ocean that helps you drink and breathe. Log a glass, watch the tide rise, breathe with the swell. Ambient music synthesized live in the browser. |
| 📜 **The Hidden Sea** — *see clearly* | [`the-hidden-sea.html`](the-hidden-sea.html) | The water you can't see. Assemble an ordinary day and watch the hidden "virtual water" behind it rise. |
| 🌅 **The Ripple** — *act together* | [`the-ripple.html`](the-ripple.html) | One drop, multiplied. Choose commitments, watch the dawn rise, see a single choice become a sea when others join. Carry a pledge with you. |

## A note on the numbers

Figures in *The Hidden Sea* and *The Ripple* are approximate, honest orders of magnitude drawn from public water-use and virtual-water data (Water Footprint Network; Mekonnen & Hoekstra; municipal water-efficiency guidance). They vary widely by region, diet, and household — windows, not exact ledgers.
